Microfluidic Displacement Testing

Following pre-screening, promising CEOR candidates undergo quantitative evaluation in micromodels that replicate key reservoir features. These models capture geological variability by adjusting fracture density, permeability contrast, and pore structure ‚Äî conditions difficult to control in conventional corefloods.

This approach enables fast, repeatable flow tests without real core material. We provide independent analysis and formulation recommendations tailored to your specific reservoir conditions and recovery goals.
